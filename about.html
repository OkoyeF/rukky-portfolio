Hi, I'm Rukky

I'm a passionate graphic designer with a love for creating visual stories that connect with people. My journey in design started with a simple sketchbook and evolved into a career where I help brands find their unique voice through compelling visuals.

I specialize in brand identity, illustration, and print design. Whether it's crafting a memorable logo, designing eye-catching packaging, or creating custom illustrations, I approach each project with creativity, attention to detail, and a commitment to excellence.

When I'm not designing, you'll find me exploring art galleries, sketching in coffee shops, or searching for inspiration in everyday moments. I believe great design comes from being curious about the world around us.
